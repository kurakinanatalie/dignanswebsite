About us

Dignan’s is a high quality outside catering company based in Edinburgh. We have been established for five years and work from a café/brassiere which is the other side of our business.

We delight in using only the best ingredients, always from fantastic suppliers across Scotland and beyond. Customer service and fantastic food are our priority, from dealing with your initial enquiry all the way through to our event staff looking after your every need.

We have a dedicated team of staff with a wealth of hospitality experience, who are ready and waiting to help your event run seamlessly. The environment and sustainability are key drivers for us, so please don't hesitate to ask how we can take this into consideration while planning your event.

All our menus are designed to give you an idea of our style and prices. They are not exhaustive and can be tailored in any way to suit your specific requirements.

We are offering office lunches, corporate catering, corporate barbeques, etc in and around Edinburgh.

Our customers can order simple and/or repeat catering by e-mail. This would require the customers to register their details before being allowed to order using this method.
